Common questions

Where is my data? On your device. GlowUp has no accounts and no servers — see the privacy policy.

Do my photos leave my phone? No, unless you tap "Back up to Photos", which copies them into your own photo library.

What happens if I delete the app? Your data is deleted with it. Use "Back up to Photos" for progress photos you want to keep, and note that your own iCloud/device backups may also preserve app data.

Is this medical advice? No — GlowUp offers cosmetic guidance only. Please consult a dermatologist for skin concerns.
