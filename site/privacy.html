GlowUp — Privacy Policy

Effective July 12, 2026

The short version: GlowUp has no accounts, no analytics, no ads, and no tracking of any kind. Everything you put into the app — routines, check-offs, reminders, quiz answers, shopping list, and progress photos — stays on your device. We never see it, and neither does anyone else.

What data GlowUp stores

GlowUp stores your skincare routines, daily check-offs, reminders, quiz answers, shopping list, and progress photos locally on your device only. Progress photos are kept in the app's private storage and are encrypted at rest whenever your phone is locked. Nothing is uploaded to us — GlowUp operates no servers and collects no data.

What GlowUp never does

  • No account or sign-up — we can't identify you.
  • No analytics, telemetry, or crash-reporting SDKs.
  • No advertising and no data sharing or selling — there is nothing to share.

Permissions GlowUp asks for

Camera — to take progress photos, stored privately on your device. Photo library (add-only) — only if you export a timelapse or use the optional "Back up to Photos" button; copies then live in your own photo library under your control. Notifications — reminders and step timers are scheduled locally on your device; no notification data leaves your phone.

Backups

If you use iCloud Backup or back your phone up to a computer, your device may include GlowUp's data in that backup, under your own Apple account and control. The optional "Back up to Photos" feature copies progress photos into your personal photo library, which may sync with your own iCloud Photos.

Store links

Product pages in the Shop can open retailer websites (such as Amazon) in your browser. Once you leave the app, the retailer's own privacy policy applies. As an Amazon Associate, GlowUp earns from qualifying purchases — this happens through the link itself and involves no personal data from the app.

Not medical advice

GlowUp offers cosmetic guidance and habit tracking only. It is not medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Consult a dermatologist for skin concerns.

Children

GlowUp is not directed at children under 13.

Changes

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted at this address with a new effective date. Because GlowUp collects nothing, changes would only ever describe new optional features.
